Psychedelic services

Integration focused support, with safety and structure

Psychedelic experiences can be powerful. Integration helps you make sense of what surfaced and translate insight into real change through therapy, reflection, and daily practices.

What integration can include

Meditation, journaling, mindful movement, time in nature, music, and structured therapy sessions. Integration looks different for everyone, because your experience is unique.

The aim is greater balance and wholeness internally and externally, in relationships, lifestyle, and how you relate to yourself.

A note on scope

We provide psychotherapy and integration support. Any medical decisions, prescribing, or administration of substances must happen in appropriate licensed settings.

If you are unsure what applies to you, contact us and we will clarify the safest path forward.

Individual psychedelic therapy

Personalized sessions to prepare, integrate, and build an integrated plan for full body wellness.

Psychedelic group ceremonies

Group support focused on reflection, grounding, and integration, with a clear structure and care.

Psychedelic medicine consultations

Clarity on goals, readiness, risks, and integration planning, with referrals when appropriate.

For providers

Coaching consultation, clinical supervision, and training aligned with ethical, client centered care.

Ketamine

Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic used in medical settings for decades. In supervised settings it can be used as part of mental health treatment and may bring rapid relief for some people.

When paired with psychotherapy, it can support processing emotions and memories and help integrate insight into everyday life.

Psilocybin

Psilocybin is the active compound in magic mushrooms. Research suggests potential for depression, trauma related symptoms, addiction, and more, by supporting changes in perspective and emotional processing.

Legal status varies by location. We can help you understand safe, lawful options and focus on integration.
