Psychedelic services

Integration that turns insight into change

Psychedelic assisted psychotherapy is an emerging approach combining psychedelics and psychotherapy to support conditions such as PTSD and treatment resistant depression.

What are the effects of psychedelics

Psychedelic experiences can feel transformational through shifts in perspective and emotional release. Integration in the days and weeks after can help you maximize benefits in a grounded way.

Integration can include practices like meditation, walking, journaling, and music, tailored to you and your life.

Psychedelic assisted therapy

Through personalized coaching, we build an integrated plan for mind and body wellness, helping you process what came up and translate it into healthier patterns.

Individual psychedelic therapy Group ceremonies Medicine consultations

Ketamine

Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic used in hospitals for decades and has shown effectiveness for certain mental health conditions in medically supervised settings.

When paired with psychotherapy, it can help you process difficult emotions and memories and support longer term improvement.

Psilocybin

Psilocybin, the active compound in magic mushrooms, is a well known psychedelic being studied for therapeutic potential across multiple conditions.

A key focus is integration, helping you build healthier meaning and choices after the experience.
