Psychedelic assisted therapy

Integration focused support

Psychedelic assisted psychotherapy is an emerging approach combining psychotherapy with carefully supported experiences. Integration is where insights become lasting change.

What integration can look like

Reflection in the days and weeks after an experience helps you carry meaning into everyday life. We tailor the process to you, using practices like mindfulness, journaling, movement, and values based planning.

Mindful awareness Self compassion Grounding practices Values clarity

Why people seek this work

Many people describe perspective shifts, emotional release, and a renewed sense of connection. A trained therapist helps you process what surfaced and translate it into actionable steps.

Ketamine

Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic used in hospitals for decades. In medically supervised settings it can support relief for certain conditions, sometimes within hours. When paired with psychotherapy, it can help process difficult emotions and create durable change.

Psilocybin

Psilocybin is the active compound in so called magic mushrooms. Research suggests potential benefits across depression, PTSD, addiction, and more. Integration is essential, because insight becomes valuable when it changes how you live.
