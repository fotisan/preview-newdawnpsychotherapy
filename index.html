Modalities

Ketamine has been used medically for decades. In medically supervised settings it is legal and can provide benefits quickly. When paired with psychotherapy, it can support emotional processing and lasting improvements through integration.

Psilocybin is a well known psychedelic being studied for therapeutic potential. Research suggests it may support conditions like depression, PTSD, and addiction by shifting patterns and supporting new perspectives, with integration as the anchor.
