Psychedelic Services

Psychedelic assisted psychotherapy is an emerging approach that combines carefully held experiences with therapeutic integration.

What are the effects of psychedelics

Psychedelic experiences can shift perspective and create insight, emotional release, and new meaning. Integration helps you translate what came up into real life.

Integration can include therapeutic reflection and grounded practices like meditation, walking, journaling, and music. Each person’s process is different.

Offerings

  • Individual psychedelic therapy
  • Psychedelic group ceremonies
  • Psychedelic medicine consultations

For providers

  • Coaching consultation
  • Clinical supervision
  • Psychedelic training

Ketamine

Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic used in medical settings for decades. In a supervised context, it can provide rapid symptom relief for some people.

When paired with psychotherapy, ketamine can support emotional processing and durable shifts in daily functioning.

Psilocybin

Psilocybin is the active compound in “magic mushrooms.” Research suggests therapeutic potential for depression, trauma, and addiction through changes in brain connectivity and meaning making.
