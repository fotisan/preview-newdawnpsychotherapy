New Frontiers

Psychedelic Services

Emerging therapeutic approaches combining medicine and psychotherapy for profound insight.

Ketamine Assisted Therapy

Ketamine has proven effective in treating mental health conditions. When paired with psychotherapy, it can help process difficult emotions and memories, leading to lasting improvements.

Psilocybin Integration

Psilocybin can lead to profound changes in consciousness. Research indicates it may effectively treat depression and PTSD by fostering neural connections that improve mood.
