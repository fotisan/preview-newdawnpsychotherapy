Psychedelic assisted therapy

Integration focused support

Psychedelic assisted psychotherapy is an emerging approach combining psychotherapy with carefully supported experiences.

What integration can look like

Reflection in the days and weeks after an experience helps you carry meaning into everyday life.

Mindfulness Self compassion Grounding Values

Why people seek this work

Many people describe perspective shifts and a renewed sense of connection. A trained therapist helps you process what surfaced and translate it into actionable steps.

Ketamine

Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic used in medically supervised settings. When paired with psychotherapy, it can help process difficult emotions.

Psilocybin

Psilocybin is the active compound in "magic mushrooms." Integration is essential, because insight becomes valuable when it changes how you live.
